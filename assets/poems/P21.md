Your laughter is a song I want to keep,
Within the hidden chambers of my heart.
It wakes me from the darkness of my sleep,
And gives me reasons for a brand new start.
Your touch is softer than a summer breeze,
That whispers through the tall and ancient trees.

The stars are jealous of the light you hold,
A radiance that warms the coldest room.
It turns the grey and dusty world to gold,
And chases out the shadows and the gloom.
I’m caught within the orbit of your grace,
And find my home within your kind embrace.

The years will pass like seasons on the vine,
But love will bloom as bright as it is now.
I’m thankful that your path has crossed with mine,
And made this deep and everlasting vow.
We walk together hand in steady hand,
Across the shifting and the golden sand.
