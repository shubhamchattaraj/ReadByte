The stars are cold and distant in the deep,
A vast expanse where silent echoes fly.
The secrets that the ancient shadows keep,
Are hidden far beneath the velvet sky.
I call into the dark and hear no sound,
Just empty space where nothing can be found.

The gravity is pulling at my heart,
A tether breaking in the endless blue.
The universe is tearing us apart,
As all the old is replaced by the new.
The light is bending at the outer edge,
Where darkness sits upon a lonely ledge.

A pulse of energy begins to flare,
A ripple through the fabric of the night.
There is a charge of static in the air,
A momentary blink of blinding light.
The void consumes the remnants of the day,
And sweeps the scattered atoms far away.

The cycle turns without a single pause,
A cosmic rhythm beating in the dark.
It follows all the universal laws,
Without a need to leave a lasting mark.
I float within the silence of the sea,
Where I am nothing, yet I am set free.
