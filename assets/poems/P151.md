There is a static that has never ended,
a hum beneath the surface of the years,
it plays on channels no one's ever tended,
persistent through the silence, hopes, and fears.

I've heard the long static behind the music,
underneath the words we choose to say,
it isn't broken, isn't even skittish,
it just continues, patient, come what may.

They say all signals fade if left unguarded,
that static, too, will eventually cease,
but this one's lasted longer than expected,
outlasting every silence, every peace.

So let the long static hum forever,
a background hush to everything we do,
some noise was never meant to end or sever,
it only means the signal's coming through.
