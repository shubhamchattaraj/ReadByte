The piano keys are ivory and black,
A range of sound that echoes in the hall.
There is no way of turning safely back,
From hearing every single phantom call.
The music is a storm within the room,
That drives away the shadow and the gloom.

The fingers dance upon the weighted keys,
With precision born of years of practice spent.
They speak of all the lands across the seas,
And every moment that the heart has lent.
The sound is resonant and very deep,
To wake the dreams that long have been in sleep.

I listen to the melody unfold,
And feel the emotion rising in my chest.
The stories of the heroes that were told,
Are finding now a moment for their rest.
The piano is a voice within the dark,
That leaves upon my heart a lasting mark.

The final chord is ringing in the air,
A resonant and beautiful decay.
It leaves the spirit feeling very bare,
Before the light of ending of the day.
The piano is silent in the night,
To wait until the morning brings the light.
