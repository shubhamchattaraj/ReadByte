I ran away in colors black as sable,
a fugitive from every waking light,
I left no note, no forwarding, no label,
just vanished soft into the welcome night.

They'll search the roads for someone dressed in shadow,
but shadow doesn't leave a trail to find,
I've learned to move like darkness through the meadow,
a sable runaway, both free and blind.

Sometimes escape don't need a destination,
just distance from the place that held you tight,
I've built my whole and quiet liberation,
from walking sable-colored through the night.

So let them post the signs, the search, the worry,
I'm already gone, already out of sight,
a sable runaway is in no hurry,
to be found again beneath the waking light.
