The nebula is spinning in the dark,
A cloud of color born from dying suns.
We catch a little of the phantom spark,
Before the thread of human life unspins.
The gravity is bending every light,
Into the beauty of the endless night.

We sail upon the winds of solar fire,
Beyond the reach of everything we know.
Our journey is a burning, deep desire,
To see the ways the distant galaxies grow.
The distance is a chasm wide and deep,
Where promises of future empires sleep.

The stardust clings to metal and to bone,
A reminder of the origin of all.
We travel through the darkness all alone,
To answer to a cosmic, final call.
The universe is expanding in the space,
To show us all the wonder of its face.

The drift is silent as a falling star,
Across the velvet blanket of the sky.
We wonder where the other travelers are,
And ask the gods of silence for a why.
The stellar light is fading into grey,
As we are swept into the far away.
