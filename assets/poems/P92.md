There's a red current running underneath,
a crimson undertow beneath the calm,
it pulls at everything that dares to breathe
too close to shores that offer no alarm.

I've felt it in the quiet after arguments,
the way the red still churns beneath the peace,
it doesn't show itself in loud laments,
it only waits until the waters cease.

I've learned to watch for red beneath the water,
the subtle warning colors sometimes give,
a crimson undertow will always slaughter
the calm you thought you had enough to live.

So watch the water closely when it's still,
don't trust the surface just because it's blue,
the crimson undertow has its own will,
and pulls the quietest shores completely through.
