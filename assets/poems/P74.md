There's power humming underneath the calm,
a quiet voltage running through my chest,
it doesn't shout, it doesn't raise an alarm,
it only waits, electric, unexpressed.

I've felt it in the pause before I speak,
the charge that builds in silence, thick and slow,
it isn't loud, but neither is it weak,
it's just a current few will ever know.

The voltage doesn't need to prove its strength,
it doesn't flash to show you it exists,
it simply waits, however short or length,
until the moment when it can't resist.

So don't mistake my quiet for the weak,
there's current running underneath my skin,
one day the silence finally will speak,
and let the quiet voltage in.
