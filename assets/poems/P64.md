Between the channels something learns to grow,
a flower built from silence and from noise,
it opens where the broken signals go,
and hums a tune with neither words nor voice.

I found it once behind the midnight screen,
a garden made of gray and flickering light,
its petals shift from black to silver sheen,
it blooms most bright in the emptiest night.

They say the noise is nothing, just decay,
the ghost of signals sent and never claimed,
but I have watched that flower turn to day,
and open wide though no one called its name.

So if you find the dial stuck between,
don't rush to turn it back to something known,
just watch the bloom of silver and of gray screen,
and let the static flower on its own.
