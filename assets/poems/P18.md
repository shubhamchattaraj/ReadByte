The mountain peaks are crowned with eternal snow,
Where dragons dream of gold and ancient lore.
The fires deep within the earth still glow,
Beside the heavy, stone-encrusted door.
The mages gather beneath the silver tree,
To seek the truth of what is meant to be.

A sword of starlight forged in cooling rain,
Is lifted high against the encroaching dark.
It carries all the sorrow and the pain,
And leaves upon the sky a burning mark.
The kingdoms rise and fall like shifting sand,
Across the breadth of this forgotten land.

The prophecy is carved upon the wall,
In runes that shimmer with a mystic light.
The heroes answer to the bugle call,
And charge into the heart of endless fight.
The magic flows like water in the streams,
To fuel the courage of our wildest dreams.
